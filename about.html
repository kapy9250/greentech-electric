About GreenTech Electric

Energy efficiency experts serving the San Francisco Bay Area since 2011

Our Story

Founded in 2011 by Manuel Cortinas, GreenTech Electric was built on a simple yet powerful vision: to provide energy-efficient electrical solutions that save money without compromising quality.

As a licensed electrical contractor serving the San Francisco Bay Area, we specialize in interior lighting design and energy conservation for industrial, commercial, retail, and non-profit organizations.

With over a decade of experience and more than 500 successful projects, we've proven that sustainable electrical solutions deliver real value to our clients while protecting our environment.
