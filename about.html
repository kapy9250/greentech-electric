Our Story

Founded in 2011 by Manuel Cortinas, GreenTech Electric was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to make sustainable electrical solutions accessible and affordable for businesses and homeowners throughout the San Francisco Bay Area.

What started as a commitment to quality craftsmanship has evolved into a full-service electrical contracting firm specializing in energy-efficient systems. We've built our reputation project by project, earning the trust of industrial, commercial, retail, and non-profit clients who value expertise, integrity, and innovation.

As a licensed electrical contractor, we stay at the forefront of industry advancements—from cutting-edge EV charging infrastructure to smart building systems. Our philosophy remains unchanged: going green doesn't have to cost you green.
