About GreenTech Electric

Building lasting partnerships through exceptional service and innovative electrical solutions since 2010.

Our Story

GreenTech Electric was founded in 2010 with a clear mission: to provide Southern California's commercial and multi-family properties with electrical services that combine technical excellence, environmental responsibility, and exceptional customer care.

What started as a small team of dedicated electricians has grown into a trusted partner for property managers, developers, and business owners throughout the region. Our growth reflects our commitment to quality, safety, and innovation in every project we undertake.

Today, we're proud to have completed over 500 successful projects, from small tenant improvements to major infrastructure upgrades, always maintaining the same level of attention to detail and customer service that defined our earliest work.

Why Choose GreenTech Electric?

What sets us apart in the commercial electrical industry

Commercial & Multi-Family Specialists

Unlike general electricians, we focus exclusively on commercial and multi-family properties. This specialization means we understand the unique challenges of working in occupied buildings, coordinating with property managers, and meeting the specific codes and requirements of your property type.

Licensed, Bonded & Insured

We maintain all required licensing (C-10 Electrical Contractor), comprehensive insurance coverage, and bonds to protect your investment and provide peace of mind.

24/7 Emergency Response

Electrical emergencies don't follow business hours. That's why we offer round-the-clock emergency services to our commercial clients, ensuring rapid response when you need it most.

Competitive & Transparent Pricing

We provide detailed, itemized quotes with no hidden fees. Our pricing is competitive, and we work with your budget to deliver maximum value.
