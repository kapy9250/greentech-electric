EV Charging Levels

Choose the right charging solution for your needs

Level 1 Charging

120V

Basic charging using standard household outlets. Ideal for overnight residential charging.

  • 3-5 miles of range per hour
  • Standard 120V outlet
  • Cost-effective installation

Level 2 Charging

240V

Most popular option for home and business. Faster charging with dedicated circuit.

  • 12-60 miles of range per hour
  • 240V dedicated circuit
  • Best value for most users

Recommended for residential & commercial

Level 3 Charging

480V

DC fast charging for commercial applications. Rapid charging for fleet operations.

  • 60-100+ miles in 20 minutes
  • 480V DC power
  • Commercial applications
