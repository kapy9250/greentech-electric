Electric Vehicle Charging Solutions

As electric vehicle adoption accelerates, providing EV charging infrastructure is rapidly becoming essential for competitive multi-family and commercial properties. GreenTech Electric specializes in designing and installing scalable EV charging systems that meet current demand while allowing for future expansion.

From initial electrical assessment through installation, permitting, and ongoing support, we handle every aspect of your EV charging project. Our solutions are tailored to your property type, electrical capacity, and budget.

Our EV Charging Services

  • Site Assessment & Load Analysis - Evaluate existing electrical capacity and charging needs
  • Level 2 Charging Stations - 240V chargers for residential and commercial use
  • Multi-Unit Systems - Scalable installations with individual metering
  • Load Management Solutions - Smart systems that optimize power distribution
  • Network Integration - Connect to billing and management platforms
  • Panel Upgrades - Expand electrical capacity to support charging infrastructure
  • Trenching & Conduit - Underground infrastructure for parking areas
  • Permitting & Compliance - Handle all permitting and code requirements

Load Management Technology

One of the biggest challenges in EV charging installations is managing electrical demand. Our load management systems intelligently distribute available power among multiple charging stations, allowing you to install more chargers without expensive utility upgrades. These smart systems can:

  • Automatically balance power across all chargers
  • Prioritize charging based on time or tenant needs
  • Prevent overloading your electrical system
  • Reduce installation costs by maximizing existing capacity

Incentives & Rebates

California offers some of the nation's best incentives for EV charging infrastructure. Available programs include:

  • Utility rebates covering equipment and installation costs
  • California Electric Vehicle Infrastructure Project (CALeVIP) funding
  • Low Carbon Fuel Standard (LCFS) credits
  • Federal tax credits for commercial installations

We help you navigate these programs and maximize your incentives, often offsetting 40-60% of total project costs.
