EV Charging Levels Explained

Not all charging stations are created equal. Understanding the differences helps you choose the right solution.

Level 1

120V Standard Outlet

Uses a standard household outlet. Ideal for overnight charging at home. Adds about 3-5 miles of range per hour.

Best for: Homeowners with low daily mileage, plug-in hybrids.

Level 2

240V Dedicated Circuit

Our most popular option. Requires dedicated 240V circuit (similar to electric dryer). Adds 12-80 miles of range per hour depending on amperage.

Best for: Residential driveways, office parking, retail locations, hospitality.

Level 3 (DC Fast)

480V+ DC Fast Charging

High-power DC charging for commercial applications. Can add 100-200 miles in 15-30 minutes. Requires significant electrical infrastructure.

Best for: Highway rest stops, fleet operations, public charging networks.
