Parking Lot Lighting

Energy-efficient LED parking lot lighting that enhances security and reduces costs

Professional Parking Lot Lighting Solutions

Proper parking lot lighting is essential for tenant safety, liability reduction, and property appeal. However, traditional parking lot lighting consumes massive amounts of energy and requires frequent maintenance. GreenTech Electric specializes in LED parking lot lighting retrofits and new installations that dramatically reduce energy costs while providing superior illumination.

Our parking lot lighting solutions are designed to meet all applicable codes and standards while maximizing efficiency, minimizing light pollution, and reducing your total cost of ownership.

Our Parking Lot Lighting Services

  • LED Retrofit Conversions - Upgrade existing fixtures to high-efficiency LED technology
  • New Lighting Installations - Complete parking lot lighting design and installation
  • Photocell Controls - Automatic dusk-to-dawn operation
  • Motion Sensors - Adaptive lighting that responds to activity
  • Lighting Layouts - Professional photometric analysis and design
  • Underground Wiring - Trenching, conduit, and electrical distribution
  • Pole Installation & Repair - New poles, pole bases, and structural repairs
  • Code Compliance Assessment - Evaluate current lighting against requirements

Benefits of LED Parking Lot Lighting

  • Energy Savings - Reduce energy consumption by 60-75% compared to metal halide or HPS
  • Lower Maintenance - LED fixtures last 50,000+ hours (10-15 years typical lifespan)
  • Improved Safety - Better light quality and color rendering enhance visibility
  • Instant Operation - No warm-up time; full brightness immediately
  • Reduced Light Pollution - Directional LEDs minimize sky glow and spill light
  • Enhanced Property Appeal - Modern, clean lighting improves first impressions
  • Lower Operating Costs - Reduced energy and maintenance expenses
  • Utility Rebates - Significant rebates available for LED conversions

Design Standards & Compliance

Parking lot lighting must meet specific standards for safety and accessibility:

  • IESNA Standards - Illuminating Engineering Society recommended practices
  • Local Codes - Municipal lighting ordinances and requirements
  • ADA Compliance - Proper illumination of accessible parking and pathways
  • Light Trespass - Minimize impact on adjacent properties
  • Uniformity Ratios - Consistent lighting levels to prevent dark spots

Our professional lighting designs include full photometric analysis showing footcandle levels, uniformity ratios, and compliance with all applicable standards.

Types of Parking Facilities We Serve

  • Surface Parking Lots - Open parking areas for apartments, offices, and retail
  • Multi-Level Parking Structures - Covered and enclosed parking garages
  • Access Drives & Roadways - Perimeter roads and entry/exit routes
  • Loading Areas - Service and delivery zones requiring specialized lighting
  • Pedestrian Walkways - Paths connecting parking to buildings

Retrofit vs. New Installation

LED Retrofits are ideal when existing poles and electrical infrastructure are in good condition. We can often retrofit existing fixtures with LED technology at a fraction of the cost of complete replacement, with similar energy savings and performance.

New Installations are recommended when poles are damaged, insufficient lighting coverage exists, or you're developing new parking areas. We handle all aspects including electrical engineering, permitting, trenching, and installation.

Smart Controls & Automation

Modern parking lot lighting can be intelligently controlled to maximize savings:

  • Photocells - Automatically turn lights on at dusk and off at dawn
  • Timers - Schedule lighting based on actual usage patterns
  • Motion Sensors - Dim lights when no activity is detected, full brightness on motion
  • Networked Controls - Centrally manage and monitor entire lighting systems
  • Dimming Schedules - Reduce brightness during low-traffic hours

Utility Rebates & ROI

LED parking lot lighting projects typically qualify for substantial utility rebates. We help you:

  • Calculate available rebate amounts (often $50-150+ per fixture)
  • Complete all rebate applications and documentation
  • Coordinate utility inspections if required
  • Maximize your return on investment

With energy savings, reduced maintenance, and utility rebates combined, most parking lot LED conversions pay for themselves in 2-4 years.
