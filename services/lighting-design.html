Lighting Design & Installation

Transform your property with energy-efficient LED lighting that reduces costs and enhances safety

Professional Lighting Solutions

Lighting is one of the most impactful and cost-effective upgrades you can make to your commercial or multi-family property. Our expert team designs and installs energy-efficient LED lighting systems that can reduce your energy costs by up to 75% while improving visibility, safety, and curb appeal.

Whether you're looking to retrofit existing fixtures, completely redesign your lighting layout, or add new lighting to previously dark areas, GreenTech Electric delivers solutions that balance aesthetics, functionality, and efficiency.

Our Lighting Services

  • LED Retrofits & Upgrades - Convert existing fixtures to energy-efficient LED technology
  • Interior Common Area Lighting - Hallways, lobbies, stairwells, and amenity spaces
  • Exterior & Perimeter Lighting - Building facades, pathways, and security lighting
  • Parking Structure Lighting - Multi-level garages and covered parking areas
  • Landscape & Accent Lighting - Enhance landscaping and architectural features
  • Emergency & Exit Lighting - Code-compliant safety lighting systems
  • Smart Lighting Controls - Automated systems with scheduling and dimming
  • Photocell & Motion Sensors - Intelligent lighting that reduces waste

Benefits of LED Lighting Upgrades

  • Energy savings of 50-75% compared to traditional lighting
  • Longer lifespan (50,000+ hours) reduces maintenance costs
  • Improved light quality with better color rendering
  • Instant-on performance with no warm-up time
  • Reduced heat output improves HVAC efficiency
  • Available utility rebates and incentives
  • Enhanced safety and security through better illumination
  • Increased property value and tenant satisfaction

Our Design Process

Every property has unique lighting needs based on its layout, usage patterns, and aesthetic goals. Our process ensures optimal results:

  • Site Assessment - We evaluate your current lighting, measure light levels, and identify problem areas
  • Custom Design - Our team creates a lighting plan that meets code requirements while optimizing efficiency and aesthetics
  • ROI Analysis - We provide detailed cost-benefit analysis showing energy savings and payback period
  • Professional Installation - Licensed electricians install all fixtures with minimal disruption to your operations
  • Testing & Training - We verify all systems work properly and train your staff on controls and maintenance

Rebates & Incentives

Many utility companies offer significant rebates for LED lighting upgrades. We help you navigate the incentive process, complete necessary paperwork, and maximize your return on investment. In many cases, rebates can cover 20-40% of project costs.
