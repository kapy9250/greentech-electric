Commercial Electrical System Upgrades

Aging electrical infrastructure is a leading cause of business interruptions, safety hazards, and energy inefficiency. GreenTech Electric specializes in comprehensive electrical renovations for commercial and multi-family properties. We help you proactively upgrade your systems to meet current codes, support modern equipment loads, and ensure the safety of your tenants and employees.

Whether you're dealing with flickering lights, tripping breakers, or simply need more power for new equipment, our team can assess your current system and recommend the most cost-effective path to modernization.

Our Renovation Services

  • Main Service Panel Upgrades - Increase capacity (e.g., 200A to 800A+)
  • Subpanel Installation & Replacement - Distribute power safely throughout your building
  • Complete Building Rewiring - Replace old, unsafe wiring (knob-and-tube, aluminum)
  • Circuit Additions - Dedicated power for servers, machinery, or HVAC
  • Code Compliance Corrections - Fix violations and pass inspections
  • Transformer Installation - Step-down or step-up transformers for commercial power
  • Grounding & Bonding - Improve safety and protect sensitive electronics
  • Surge Protection - Whole-building protection against power spikes

Signs You Need an Upgrade

If your property is more than 20-30 years old and hasn't had a major electrical update, look for these warning signs:

  • Circuit breakers trip frequently
  • Lights flicker or dim when equipment turns on
  • Outlets feel warm or look discolored
  • You rely heavily on extension cords
  • You can't add new equipment due to lack of power
  • Insurance companies require an inspection or upgrade

The Renovation Process

We understand that electrical work can be disruptive. Our process is designed to minimize downtime:

  • Assessment - Thorough audit of your existing system and load calculations
  • Planning - Detailed scope of work including phasing plan to keep you operational
  • Permitting - We handle all engineering, drawings, and city permits
  • Execution - Scheduled work hours (including nights/weekends if needed)
  • Coordination - Seamless cooperation with utility companies (SCE, LADWP, etc.)
  • Cutover - Carefully planned power switchover to minimize outage time

Multi-Family Specific Renovations

For apartment complexes and HOAs, we offer specialized renovation programs:

  • Unit Panel Upgrades - Replacing Federal Pacific or Zinsco panels
  • Meter Bank Replacement - Upgrading deteriorated utility metering equipment
  • Common Area Upgrades - House power panels and distribution
  • EV Readiness - Pre-wiring for future charger installation
