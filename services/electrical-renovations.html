Signs You Need an Electrical Renovation

Don't wait for a failure. Watch for these warning signs.

Tripping Breakers

Frequent circuit breaker trips indicate that your system is overloaded and struggling to handle current power demands.

Flickering Lights

Lights that dim or flicker when appliances turn on suggest voltage fluctuations or loose connections that need immediate attention.

Building Age

Commercial buildings over 20 years old often lack the grounding and capacity required for modern electronics and safety standards.

Renovation Plans

Adding square footage, new equipment, or EV chargers? You'll likely need a service upgrade to support the additional load.
