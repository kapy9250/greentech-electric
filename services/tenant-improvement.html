Professional Tenant Improvement Services

Tenant improvements (TI) are critical moments in the life of a commercial property. Whether you're preparing space for a new tenant or upgrading an existing unit, the electrical work must be completed on time, on budget, and to code. GreenTech Electric specializes in tenant improvement electrical work, with extensive experience coordinating with general contractors, architects, and property managers to deliver seamless results.

We understand the importance of minimizing vacancy time while ensuring quality workmanship. Our project management approach keeps your TI projects moving forward efficiently, with clear communication at every stage.

Our Tenant Improvement Services

  • Complete Electrical Buildouts - Full electrical installation for new tenant spaces
  • Power Distribution - New circuits, panels, and subpanels as needed
  • Lighting Design & Installation - Custom lighting plans for retail, office, or restaurant spaces
  • Data & Communication Wiring - Structured cabling, network infrastructure, and phone systems
  • HVAC Electrical - Power for new or relocated heating and cooling systems
  • Kitchen & Specialized Equipment - High-amperage circuits for commercial kitchens and equipment
  • Code Compliance Upgrades - Bring older spaces up to current electrical codes
  • Emergency & Exit Lighting - Code-required safety lighting systems

Types of Spaces We Serve

  • Retail Spaces - Display lighting, point-of-sale systems, security, and storefront illumination
  • Office Suites - Workstation power, task lighting, conference rooms, and IT infrastructure
  • Restaurants & Cafes - Commercial kitchen power, dining area ambiance, outdoor dining lighting
  • Medical Offices - Exam room lighting, specialized equipment power, patient comfort systems
  • Fitness Centers - High-bay lighting, equipment power, locker room facilities
  • Salons & Spas - Styling station power, ambient lighting, water heater circuits

Our Process

  • Initial Consultation - Review plans, understand tenant requirements, and identify electrical scope
  • Detailed Estimate - Itemized quote based on architectural plans and specifications
  • Permit Coordination - Handle all electrical permits and inspector coordination
  • GC Collaboration - Work seamlessly with general contractors and other trades
  • Phased Installation - Coordinate rough-in, trim-out, and final installation phases
  • Testing & Inspection - Ensure all work passes inspection on first submission
  • Punch List & Closeout - Address any final items and provide as-built documentation

Why TI Work Is Different

Tenant improvement electrical work requires a unique skill set and approach:

  • Timeline Pressure - Vacancy costs money; we work efficiently to meet tight schedules
  • Coordination Intensive - Multiple trades working simultaneously require careful planning
  • Existing Building Challenges - Working within existing structures often requires creative solutions
  • Code Compliance - Bringing older buildings up to current codes while respecting budgets
  • Tenant Satisfaction - The end result must meet both landlord and tenant expectations

Cost Control & Value Engineering

We work within your TI budget by offering value engineering services:

  • Identify cost-effective alternatives that maintain quality
  • Suggest phasing options to spread costs across lease terms
  • Recommend energy-efficient solutions that reduce long-term operating costs
  • Provide accurate estimates early to avoid budget surprises

  • In-house design capabilities to solve complex challenges
